My Current Manga!

Hi. My name is Diamond Dang and I'm currently obsessed over this manga called "The guy she was interested in wasn't a guy at all" by artist Sumiko Arai! The story revolves around Aya Ooosawa, a popular high school girl who has a secret love for rock music. She had never shared her music interest to her popular friends because none of them liked the same music and she's scared she would be alienated. She loves going to record stores to shop for music on her own. Although she's not interested in boys, she developed a major crush on the guy that works at the record store who dresses super cool and shares many similar musical interests with her, with he always wears a face mask to cover his face. As it turns out, the cool guy that she has a crush on is the shy girl in the same class as her - Mitsuki Koga! Mitsuki knows Aya, but is too scared to reveal her true identity and keeps pretending to be this "cool guy" for Aya. Of course, Aya will eventually find out...

It's a simple but fun trope to read about, and what I like most about the this manga is the story telling and the relationship dynamic between the two! They also have an official playlist of their shared favorite songs that I love to listen to when I'm reading this manga. They portray the cutesy but also a bit rebelious phase of teenager love very well!
